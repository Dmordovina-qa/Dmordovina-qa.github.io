Joel Miller

Biography

Joel Miller is a survivor in a post‑apocalyptic world...

Born in Texas, Joel became a hardened survivor after the Cordyceps outbreak. His past as a smuggler and loss of his daughter Sarah shaped his ruthless survival instincts.

Character & Motivation

His pragmatic nature and protective instincts define his journey...

  • Anti‑hero: Not perfect, often violates moral norms, but driven by deep personal pain.
  • Pragmatism: Chooses the most efficient (often cruel) path to achieve his goal.
  • Protective instinct: Willing to do anything for those he cares about (first for the memory of Sarah, then for Ellie).
  • Emotional detachment: Avoids forming attachments due to the fear of new losses.
  • Traumatization: Haunted by nightmares, prone to outbursts of anger, unable to talk about his past.
  • Resilience: Physical and psychological endurance forged through years of survival.
  • Stubbornness: Refuses to compromise when it comes to protecting “his people”.
